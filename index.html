GUARDIANS OF THE GALAXYVol.3

Overview:
In Marvel Studios "Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3" our beloved band of misfits are looking a bit different these days.
Peter Quill, still reeling from the loss of Gamora, must rally his team around him to defend the universe along with
protecting one of their own. A mission that, if not completed successfully, could quite possibly lead to the end of the
Guardians as we know them.
Director: James Gunn
Cast:
Chris Pratt, Karen Gillan, Pom Klementieff, Sean Gunn, Will Poulter, Maria Bakalova and Chukwudi Iwuji
Release Date: May 5, 2023
